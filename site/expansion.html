Bring a Sanctuary to Your Community

Help us identify communities ready for the Autism Sanctuary model.

We are seeking communities with the right workforce, leadership commitment, and land context to support sustainable, nature-based DD/ID services.

Site Readiness Criteria

What we look for first.

  • Workforce proximity: 20 to 30-minute commute access to a major college or university.
  • Academic pipeline strength: nearby programs in occupational therapy, special education, psychology/social work, nursing/pre-med, and related pathways.
  • Land feasibility: practical access to suitable rural or agricultural parcels for light therapeutic farming.
  • Community alignment: local leadership and partners committed to person-centered DD/ID services.
