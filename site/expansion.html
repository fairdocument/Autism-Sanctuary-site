National Growth

Partner with us to grow care farming communities rooted in evidence-based practice.

Autism Sanctuary identifies regions where person-centered support, therapeutic horticulture, and strong workforce pipelines can sustain a licensed IDD provider. This page is for civic leaders, funders, and clinical partners exploring a new Green Care campus—not for individual service referrals (please use Admissions).

Readiness Criteria

What we evaluate first

  • Workforce ecosystem: 20–30 minute access to a major college or university with allied and clinical programs.
  • Land & Green Care fit: Parcels suitable for therapeutic agriculture, trails, and program buildings with attention to accessibility.
  • Community leadership: Partners who share a commitment to autistic adults, self-advocates, and individuals with IDD.
  • Payer & regulatory realism: Understanding that licensure, waivers, and ISP authorization vary by state and take time.
