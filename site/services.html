Services Overview

Evidence-based services designed for dignity, growth, and independence.

Autism Sanctuary serves individuals with developmental and intellectual disabilities through nature-based programming, residential supports, and workplace assistance.

Service Line 1

Group Day & Community Coaching

Monday through Friday programming with therapeutic farm activities, animal care, life-skills coaching, and structured community outings.

  • Nature-based routines and sensory-informed supports
  • Social participation and communication opportunities
  • Community engagement with local partners
Service Line 2

Residential Services

Personalized home-based supports through Sponsored Residential, In-Home Support, and Supported Living pathways.

  • Individualized care planning and implementation
  • Family collaboration and transition planning
  • Daily-living skill development for independence
Service Line 3

Workplace Assistance

Vocational training and job support for adults with higher support needs, with coaching before, during, and after job placement.

  • 1:1 workplace navigation and coaching
  • Skill building tied to local employment needs
  • Long-term retention and success supports
Service Access

How to get started.

1) Submit Intake

Start with the admissions intake form so our team can review service needs and fit.

2) Intake Review

Our admissions and program team reviews eligibility, required documentation, and care needs.

3) Family Conversation

We schedule a meeting to discuss goals, supports, and practical next steps.

4) Service Planning

Qualified applicants move into individualized planning and onboarding.
