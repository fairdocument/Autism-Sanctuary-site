Workforce & Leadership Formation

The Carol Lynn Siemers Fellowship

The fellowship is one pillar of Autism Sanctuary’s mission: preparing emerging professionals for lifespan support through immersive person-centered support, mentorship, and reflective practice in a care farming environment. It complements—but does not replace—our core DSP workforce.

Learning through evidence-based community practice

Fellows collaborate with DSPs to facilitate meaning and motivation for autistic adults, self-advocates, and peers with IDD. Rotations span day services, residential contexts where appropriate, and pre-vocational training on trails and in therapeutic horticulture.

  • Interdisciplinary exposure aligned with nursing, SLP, psychology, social work, pre-med, and related pathways
  • Supervision emphasizing documentation, ethics, and regulatory respect
  • Commitment to neuro-inclusive language and self-advocate partnership
