Workforce and Leadership Development

The Carol Lynn Siemers Fellowship

A mission-driven fellowship that supports Autism Sanctuary's services by preparing professionals in allied and clinical pathways through direct experience, mentorship, and structured training.

One pillar within a larger model.

The fellowship is designed to strengthen quality of care while building a durable talent pipeline for DD/ID services. Fellows work alongside experienced staff in day, residential, and vocational contexts.

  • Interdisciplinary learning in applied service settings
  • Mentorship, supervision, and reflective practice
  • Alignment with nursing, speech therapy, psychology, social work, pre-med, and related pathways
Fellowship in PracticeUse existing site image assets

Clinical Exposure

Participants gain structured, real-world experience supporting individuals with DD/ID in daily care environments.

Leadership Development

Fellows build communication, team leadership, and systems awareness that transfer to long-term careers.

Community Impact

The fellowship contributes to continuity and quality across Autism Sanctuary programs.
