About Autism Sanctuary

From local vision to a national model rooted in nature and dignity.

Founded in 2020, Autism Sanctuary was created to provide meaningful, community-connected supports for individuals with developmental disabilities, especially those with higher support needs.

Our Mission

Enhancing lives through nature-based activities, community connection, and personalized support.

Autism Sanctuary is a nonprofit organization serving individuals with autism and other developmental and intellectual disabilities. We combine clinical structure, compassionate care, and therapeutic agricultural environments to support growth, belonging, and independence.

Clinical and community integrity: We maintain a person-centered approach with clear operational standards, respectful communication, and collaborative care planning.

Our Story

How Autism Sanctuary grew.

2020

Autism Sanctuary begins with a mission to create inclusive nature-based supports for individuals with developmental disabilities.

2023

Autism Sanctuary becomes a DBHDS state-licensed provider and expands service capacity.

2024-2026

Core services mature across day programming, residential supports, and workplace assistance with growing community partnerships.

Next Chapter

The Virginia site becomes the Flagship Model while Autism Sanctuary builds a coordinated national Hub-and-Spoke strategy.
