The Network

A career-long peer group, by design.

The Autism Sanctuary alumni network is structured, intentional, and active. We run quarterly virtual roundtables, annual reunion weekends at Edgefield, and a private alumni directory used heavily for residency match advice, research introductions, and clinical referrals.

  • Mentorship match. Every current fellow is paired with an alumni mentor in their target field.
  • Application support. Alumni read med-school personal statements, run mock interviews, and write context-rich letters.
  • Research pipeline. Alumni in academic medicine actively recruit junior fellows into DD/ID research projects.
  • Boomerang hires. Practicing alumni return to Edgefield as attending physicians, consulting psychologists, board members, and clinical advisors.
  • Lifetime affiliation. Once a fellow, always a fellow. We say it because we mean it operationally.
