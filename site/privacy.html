Privacy Policy

How we collect, use, and protect information.

Effective date: April 26, 2026.

Information we collect

We collect contact and form-submission data you voluntarily provide through admissions, expansion, contact, and donation inquiries.

How we use information

We use information to respond to inquiries, evaluate admissions and expansion interest, provide updates, and support service delivery and operations.

We may share data with trusted service providers that support site operations, communications, and form processing. We do not sell personal information.

We use reasonable technical and operational safeguards. We retain information only as long as needed for program operations, legal obligations, and continuity purposes.
