Privacy Policy

How we collect, use, and protect information.

Effective date: April 26, 2026.

Information we collect

We collect information that you voluntarily provide through admissions, future-location interest, employment, volunteer, donation, and general contact forms—including names, contact details, support summaries when submitted, and uploaded documents when applicable.

How we use information

We use information to respond to inquiries, coordinate supports, evaluate program fit, meet licensure and payer documentation expectations, and improve our website experience.

Sharing

We may share information with trusted vendors (e.g., email, scheduling, secure storage) under confidentiality agreements. We do not sell personal information. We disclose information when required by law or to protect safety.

Security & retention

We apply reasonable administrative, technical, and physical safeguards. Retention follows service, funding, and legal requirements.

Your choices

Request corrections or discuss privacy questions by emailing info@autismsanctuary.org.
