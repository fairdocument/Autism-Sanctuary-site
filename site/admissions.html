FAQ

General admissions questions

Who can apply for services?

Individuals and families seeking DD/ID supports through Autism Sanctuary programs can apply through the admissions intake process.

Do programs have waitlists?

Some services may have waitlists based on capacity. Admissions will communicate current timelines during review.

Is Autism Sanctuary a nonprofit and licensed provider?

Yes. Autism Sanctuary is a 501(c)(3) nonprofit and a DBHDS-licensed provider in Virginia.

What documents should I prepare?

If applicable, include the most recent ISP, behavior intervention plan, and educational or psychological evaluations from the last two years.
